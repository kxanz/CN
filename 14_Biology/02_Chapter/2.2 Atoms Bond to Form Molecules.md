Key Terms
---
**Chemical Bond** - **attraction** of two **atoms** 

**Molecule** - when two or more **atoms** come together **chemically**

**Compound** - two different **elements** come together

**Covalent Bond** 
- two atoms that share electrons to meet the configuration in the outer shell
- Each atom contributes one member of an electron pair
- **Double bond** - atoms share two electron pairs

**NonPolar bond** - **electrons** are shared equally
