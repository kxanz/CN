Key Terms 
---
**Atoms** - all matter is composed of atoms 

**Element** -a fundamental substance that consists of just one atom 
- 92 elements

Atoms consist of 3 fundamental particles:
- **Protons** - Positively charged 
- **Electrons** - Negatively charged
- **Neutrons** - uncharged 

**Protons** and **Neutrons** are together in the nucleus

**Electrons** are rapidly moving outside of the nucleus in **electron shells**

**Atomic Mass** - is measure in Daltons (Da)

---

**Fundamental elements for living systems**: 
- Hydrogen 
- oxygen 
- nitrogen
- carbon
